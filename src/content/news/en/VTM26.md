**Apply for the Board!**

Hey!

It's time to elect the D-chips board for 2026/2027. This will take place during the D-chips VTM election, which will be held on April 27th.
The positions available are Vice President, Event Manager and Head of Corporate Relations. You can apply yourself or nominate someone you think would be a good fit. Information about the positions will be published on D-Chip’s Instagram, and short descriptions are already available on D-Chip’s website. If you are nominated, you will receive an email where you can choose to book an interview.
You don’t need to commit to a specific position – nominations are not binding.

If you’re interested in board work, feel free to apply for multiple roles! During the interview, we’ll talk more about what feels like the best fit for you.

The deadline for submitting nominations is March 31th.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Elly Hedin Oleniusson via Messenger (or email: el7076he-s@user.dsek.se)
